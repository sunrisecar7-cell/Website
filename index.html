About Us

Welcome to Sunrise Car Hire, your reliable partner for car rentals on the breathtaking island of Praslin, Seychelles. Our well-maintained vehicles give you the flexibility to discover everything the island has to offer — from hidden beaches to scenic nature trails. Whether it’s a leisurely holiday, an exciting family trip, or a quick business visit, our dedicated team ensures a seamless and enjoyable ride.

Etiquette

Getting around Praslin Island is easy and enjoyable, especially with your own rental car. The island is compact, but the roads are winding and scenic, so take your time and enjoy the drive. Parking is generally available near beaches and attractions, but a little local knowledge goes a long way — as tempting as the shade may look, it’s best not to park under coconut trees. Falling coconuts are no joke and can cause serious damage to your vehicle.

Attractions

Praslin is home to some of Seychelles’ most iconic attractions and breathtaking natural beauty. A visit to the Vallée de Mai Nature Reserve, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a must, where you can walk among ancient palm forests and see the legendary coco de mer. With your own car, you can easily explore famous beaches like Anse Lazio and Anse Georgette, as well as charming villages, viewpoints, and hidden coastal gems.

7 Seater

Ideal for families, groups, and comfortable island travel.

From €75/day
A 7-seater vehicle such as a Toyota Rumion or similar is an excellent option for exploring Praslin Island with family or a group. Compact SUVs

A great balance of comfort, space, and easy island driving.

From €50/day
A compact SUV such as the Suzuki Brezza or Suzuki Fronx offers a practical step up from a standard car without feeling bulky on Praslin’s narrow island roads. With slightly higher ground clearance and a more robust feel, these vehicles handle uneven surfaces, gentle hills, and less-developed routes with confidence, while still remaining easy to maneuver and park near beaches, viewpoints, and local attractions. Compact SUVs provide added interior comfort and useful boot space for luggage or beach essentials, making them ideal for couples or small families who want a relaxed, elevated driving position and a smooth ride. They strike the perfect balance between everyday efficiency and light adventure, allowing you to explore Praslin comfortably without the size or fuel demands of a larger vehicle.
